WHO WE ARE

Who We Are

Cultivating Data Innovators

Data Science Club (DaSci) at CHARUSAT is a student-driven community dedicated to fostering a culture of learning and innovation in the fields of Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning.

We organize hands-on workshops, hackathons, expert talks, and collaborative projects to help students bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, there is a place for you here.

  • Hands-on Workshops & Seminars
  • Hackathons & Coding Competitions
  • Industry Expert Sessions
  • Peer-to-Peer Learning
