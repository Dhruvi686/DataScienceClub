Solution Challenge Hackathon 2026

Gen AI for Sustainability

23-24 March 2026
09:30 AM Onwards
A7 Building, CHARUSAT
Team Size: 1-3

The Data Science Club, CSPIT, CHARUSAT is organizing a hackathon focused on Generative AI for Sustainability. This is an exciting opportunity for all CHARUSAT students to showcase their innovation and problem-solving skills.

Students will work in teams (1–3 members) to build innovative AI-based solutions for real-world challenges related to sustainability. This hackathon aims to encourage creative thinking and practical application of generative AI technologies to address pressing environmental and social issues.

Whether you're interested in climate change, renewable energy, waste management, or other sustainability challenges, this hackathon is the perfect platform to develop and present your AI-powered solutions.
